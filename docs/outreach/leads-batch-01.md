# Leads — Batch 01 (29 verified prospects, Marrakech)

Researched & verified 2026-07-14. Every business below was checked and found to
have **no own website** (or a broken/dead one). Businesses that already had a
real site were rejected (not listed). Cross-niche accuracy spot-checked.

## How to use this (read once)

1. **5-second re-glance before sending.** Statuses move — a business could put up
   a site next week. Open their IG/listing, confirm the gap is still real, then send.
2. **Personalize the first word if you can.** If you spot the owner's name on their
   IG, open with it. Generic is fine; specific is better.
3. **Send ~10/day**, log each in `outreach-tracker.csv`, follow up **day 3 and day 8**, then stop.
4. **Voice note beats text** once someone replies — Darija or French, your call.
5. Portfolio to drop in any message: **wereact.agency/fr/work** · riad example: **kasbahangour.com**
6. ⚠️ No public phone = contact via the platform (Booking/TripAdvisor messaging) or Instagram DM.
