# How to Attract Them & Close — WeReact sales playbook

Companion to `wereact-leads.xlsx` (the 29 prospects + tracker) and
`leads-batch-01.md`. This is the *why* behind the messages: how to get a
Marrakech business owner to care, the arguments that give you the upper hand,
the launch offer, and how to close.

---

## 1. The one mindset shift

**You are not selling a website. You are selling more clients, less commission,
and being found.** Nobody wakes up wanting "a website." They want a full riad,
a busy Saturday service, a booked-out spa. Every message and every call must be
about *their* result — the website is just how you deliver it.

So every first message follows the same shape (it's already built into each lead):

1. **A specific observation about THEM** — "751 avis sur Booking", "18 000
   abonnées sans lien de réservation", "votre domaine affiche une erreur". This
   proves you did your homework and you're not spamming. It's the whole game.
2. **The hidden cost of the gap** — commission, invisibility on Google, lost DMs.
3. **Who you are + proof** — WeReact, Marrakech, portfolio link.
4. **Risk reversal — the launch offer** (see §3).
5. **A tiny ask** — "10 minutes cette semaine ?" Never "voulez-vous un site ?"

## 2. Your upper hand — why a website matters (say these)

Keep these ready; deploy the 1–2 that fit the business in front of you.

> **Master line: on Instagram or Booking, you RENT your audience. With a website,
> you OWN it.**

1. **Google = buyers with intent.** Someone typing "riad médina Marrakech" or
   "restaurant Gueliz" wants to book *now*. With no site, you're invisible to
   the highest-value customer there is.
2. **OTA commission (riads/hotels).** Booking/Expedia take 15–25% of every
   reservation. A direct-booking site pays for itself in a handful of nights.
3. **You stop depending on an algorithm.** Hacked account, shadow-ban, a rule
   that changes overnight — on your own site, none of that can wipe you out.
4. **Credibility.** A foreign tourist comparing three riads trusts the one with a
   real site over the one with only an Instagram page. A site says "serious
   business."
5. **Sells 24/7, no DM ping-pong.** Menu, prices, hours, booking — always
   available, without you answering messages one by one.
6. **Data & retargeting.** On a website we install a tracking pixel: you learn
   who visits and can re-advertise to them. Impossible with Instagram alone.
7. **Multilingual reach.** Your customers search in French *and* English. A
   bilingual site opens both markets; a single IG bio doesn't.
8. **Your competitor already has one.** Every day without a site is a booking
   that goes to whoever appears above you on Google. (Loss aversion closes deals.)

## 3. The launch offer (your closer) — personalize the numbers

A new studio's best weapon is a genuine launch offer. It creates reciprocity,
honest scarcity, and removes risk. **The offer (locked in):**

- **Product & price:** a **professional showcase site (site vitrine) for
  2000 DH** — a launch price, below the standard rate — **live in 2–3 days.**
- **Free with every site:** **SEO foundations + an optimized Google Business
  Profile.** Cheap for you to deliver, high perceived value for them.
- **Speed is the hook:** *"en ligne en 2 à 3 jours"* kills the "I don't have
  time / it'll drag on forever" objection before it's even raised. Lead with it.
- **Who:** the **first 5 businesses in Marrakech** this month. Real scarcity —
  you *do* want a few strong local references. Never fake it.
- **The clever trade:** in exchange, **an honest Google review at delivery.**
  This fixes *your* biggest weakness (zero reviews) and feeds social proof for
  the next prospect. Every client makes the next sale easier.
- **Riads / booking sites:** bigger, custom projects — offer the launch *rate*
  + the same free SEO/GBP, **not** the flat 2000 DH (a riad booking site is a
  different tier; see the pricing article ranges).
- **Why it's limited (say it):** *"parce que nous lançons notre studio et nous
  voulons quelques belles références locales."*

Why it works: **irresistible math** — fast (2–3 days) + cheap (2000 DH) + free
SEO + a launch discount leaves almost no reason to say no. Plus **reciprocity**
(you give first), **scarcity** (5 spots), and it manufactures your **review
engine** at the same time.

## 4. Objection handling (short, confident)

- **"J'ai déjà Instagram."** → Instagram, c'est louer ; un site, c'est posséder.
  Compte bloqué ou algo qui change → vous perdez tout. Et 90 % des touristes
  vérifient sur Google avant de réserver.
- **"C'est trop cher."** → Justement, tarif de lancement. Et un site se
  rembourse : pour un riad, quelques nuits sans commission ; pour un resto,
  quelques couverts en plus par mois.
- **"Je n'ai pas le temps."** → C'est nous qui faisons tout. ~30 minutes de votre
  temps au total, livraison en 2 à 4 semaines.
- **"J'ai déjà des clients."** → Parfait — pensez à ceux que vous NE captez pas
  parce qu'ils ne vous trouvent pas sur Google. De l'argent laissé sur la table
  chaque semaine.
- **"Je vais réfléchir."** → Bien sûr. Je vous garde le tarif de lancement
  quelques jours. Voici 2 exemples : wereact.agency/fr/work.

## 5. Follow-up cadence (most sales are in the follow-up)

- **Day 0:** the cold message (from the lead sheet).
- **Day 3 (no reply):** *"Bonjour 👋 juste pour m'assurer que mon message est
  bien arrivé. On aide les [type] de Marrakech à être trouvés sur Google et à
  réserver en direct, avec un tarif de lancement ce mois-ci. Un simple « oui »
  suffit 🙂 — L'équipe WeReact"*
- **Day 8 (last):** *"Bonjour 👋 dernier message pour ne pas vous déranger. Si un
  jour vous voulez un site qui ramène des clients, notre porte reste ouverte :
  wereact.agency/fr/contact. Belle continuation ! — L'équipe WeReact"*
- Then stop. Log it. Move on. (All scripts also live in the xlsx "Scripts" sheet.)

## 6. When they say "yes / c'est combien ?"

Don't quote blind. Ask 3 questions, then quote within 24h:
1. Objectif principal : être trouvé sur Google, réservations en direct, ou vendre
   en ligne ?
2. Une langue ou français + anglais ?
3. Vous avez déjà photos/textes, ou on s'en occupe ?

Then send a clear, itemized quote (ranges in the pricing article:
`/fr/blog/combien-coute-site-web-maroc`). Offer a 10-min call as the alternative.

## 7. Daily rhythm & honest numbers

- **10 messages/day**, 5 days = 50/week. Log each in the xlsx, follow up J+3/J+8.
- Realistic: ~10 % reply → ~5 conversations/week → ~1–2 quotes → your first
  client within 2–3 weeks of consistent sending.
- The compounding part: every delivered client → a Google review + a testimonial
  + referrals → the next sale gets easier. Reviews are the flywheel.

> This works alongside the paid campaign (launching ~18/07) and the SEO/blog
> already shipped. Outreach brings clients *this month*; SEO + reviews make them
> come to *you* by month three.
