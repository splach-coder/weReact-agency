# Clients Without Ads — the WeReact playbook

Built from a full repo recon (2026-07-14). Complements `seo-90-day-growth-playbook.md`
(on-site SEO — largely DONE) and the Google Ads plan (paused, launching ~18/07).
This covers everything those don't: **outreach, proof, GBP/reviews, partnerships,
referrals, marketplaces, nurture** — ordered by time-to-first-client.

---

## ⚠️ PRIORITY ZERO — fix the proof problem (before ANY outreach)

The recon found a trust landmine: the case-study pages claim **specific invented
metrics** ("35% increase in bookings", "120% organic growth in 6 months",
"250+ tour bookings") that exist nowhere in the data layer and have no client
attestation. The "testimonials" are agency-written blurbs labeled **"Real
client"** with no human names. 3 of the "7+ projects" are the agency's own
concept properties, and one live portfolio site sits on a `*.vercel.app` URL.

Why this is first: every channel below sends a skeptical Moroccan SMB owner to
your portfolio. One prospect (or worse, Kasbah Angour's actual owner) noticing
invented numbers = dead deal + reputation damage in a small city.

**Fix (1 evening):**
- [ ] Remove or source every metric. Keep only what a client confirms in writing
      or what your own analytics prove (own properties CAN show real numbers).
- [ ] Relabel testimonials honestly: "Project notes" not "Real client" — or
      better, get 2 real quotes this week (see §2).
- [ ] Label own properties as "Studio projects" — honesty reads as confidence.
- [ ] Move By Marrakech off `vercel.app` to a real domain (or drop it).
- [ ] Add the humans: founder name + photo, Marrakech address, ICE if available.
      Moroccan buyers buy from people, not brands.

## 1. WhatsApp outreach — first client in 7–14 days ⚡

The single fastest channel. Morocco runs on WhatsApp; a personalized voice-note
or message outperforms any cold email. Target 10/day, 5 days/week = 50/week.
Expect ~10–15% replies, 2–4 conversations/week, ~1–2 proposals/week.

### Target list A — riads & guesthouses bleeding commission
Booking.com charges 15–25%. A riad doing 400k MAD/year via Booking pays them
60–100k MAD — your 15–25k site pays for itself in months.
**Build the list (2h):** Booking.com → Marrakech → sort by review count 50–300
(established but small) → check each for its own website. No site, or a dead
one = target. Log in a sheet: name, contact (their Booking page → Google → phone/
Instagram), current site state.

**FR script (adapt, never paste identical):**
> Bonjour [Nom], ici l'équipe WeReact, studio web à Marrakech. Nous avons vu
> [Riad X] sur Booking — superbes avis ! Par contre je n'ai pas trouvé votre
> site de réservation directe. Chaque réservation qui passe par Booking vous
> coûte 15–25% de commission. On crée des sites de réservation directe pour
> les riads (voir kasbahangour.com). Ça vous dirait d'en parler 10 minutes ?

**EN script (for foreign-owned riads — many are French/European owned):**
> Hello [Name], this is the WeReact team, a web studio in Marrakech. We found
> [Riad X] on Booking — great reviews! But no direct-booking site. Every
> Booking reservation costs you 15–25% commission. We build direct-booking
> sites for riads (see kasbahangour.com). Worth a 10-minute chat?

### Target list B — Instagram-only businesses
Search Instagram: #marrakechrestaurant #spamarrakech #marrakechshopping
#quadmarrakech #marrakechtours. Filter: active account (posts weekly),
1k–30k followers, **no website in bio** (or a linktree). These businesses
already spend effort on digital — they understand the value.

**FR script:**
> Salam [Nom], votre page Instagram est très pro 👌 mais quand quelqu'un vous
> cherche sur Google, il ne trouve rien à réserver. On transforme des pages
> Instagram comme la vôtre en vrais sites qui prennent des réservations
> (exemples : wereact.agency/fr/work). 10 minutes cette semaine ?

### Target list C — outdated sites
Google "restaurant marrakech", "spa marrakech", "location voiture marrakech" —
pages 2–3 have businesses whose sites are old/broken/not mobile. Screenshot
their site next to one of yours; send both images. The visual does the selling.

**Rules:** personalize the first line ALWAYS · voice note after a reply
(darija/français) · follow up exactly twice (day 3, day 8) then stop ·
log every contact in HubSpot (Karim pipeline already exists).

The 90-day playbook prescribed this; recon shows **none of it verified as

- [ ] Verify GBP (listing exists — CID confirmed). NAP must match site exactly.

- [ ] Post on GBP weekly (new project, tip) — activity is a ranking signal.
- [ ] 5 clean citations: Charika.ma, Kompass Maroc, Yelo.ma, local chambers,
      any partner sites. NAP identical everywhere.

## 3. Partnerships — one good partner = deal flow forever 🤝

People who meet business owners BEFORE they need a website:
photographers/videographers (their clients need sites for content),
**fiduciaires/comptables** (every new company files through one),
coworking spaces (The Spot, Emerging Business Factory…), marketing/social-media
freelancers (sell sites, can't build them), riad management companies,
print shops (business cards → "put a real site on it").

Offer: **15% referral fee, paid on collection** — or reciprocal referrals.
Action: list 10 names you already know, WhatsApp each this week with one line:
> "Je cherche 2-3 partenaires à Marrakech : tu m'envoies des clients qui ont
> besoin d'un site, je te reverse 15%. Simple. Café cette semaine ?"

## 4. Marketplaces — cashflow while everything compounds 💶

Not glamorous; pays rent. **Malt.fr** (French SMBs love Moroccan freelancers —
same language, half the price), **Upwork** (filter: French-language jobs — far
less competition than EN), **Codeur.com** (FR-only project board). Skip Fiverr
(race to the bottom). Profile = your 3 REAL projects with honest descriptions.
Target: 5 proposals/week, personalized first paragraph. 1–2 wins/month funds
the studio while SEO matures.

## 6. The referral loop — systematize what's accidental 🔁

At delivery of EVERY project (add to Karim's HubSpot manual):
1. Ask for the Google review (link ready).
2. Ask for the 2-sentence testimonial + permission to use name/photo.
3. Offer the client 10% of any referred deal (or a free month of maintenance).
4. 30 days later: "how's the site performing?" check-in → second referral ask.

**KPIs (fill monthly):** outreach sent / replies / calls booked / proposals /
wins · GBP: reviews, calls, direction requests · GSC: FR clicks on money
queries · referrals asked/received.

## Expected timeline (honest)

- **Weeks 1–2:** proof fixed, GBP verified, first 50 outreach → first real
  conversations. Possibly first small deal.
- **Month 1:** 1–2 clients from outreach/marketplaces. 5+ Google reviews live.
- **Months 2–3:** partnerships start producing; FR content begins ranking;
  map-pack visibility for "agence web marrakech".
- **Months 4–6:** referral loop + SEO compound; outreach becomes optional.

Ads (from 18/07) accelerate this — but this system is what makes the agency
survive without them.
